About Us

Historical Background

Shamsiye Schools was established in the year 2013, with only form 1 and 2 classes. Later on in 2017, the first form five students were enrolled. This was under the first Shamsiye headmaster Mr. Alli Karakaya.

Shamsiye is located at Mbweni-Mpiji, Dar es Salaam

Education

Nursery and Primary level in Shamsiye is day, and it is provided to both sex.
Secondary and Advanced level is boarding, and it is provided to boys only.
The Advanced level teaches only PCM, PCB, EGM and CBG.
Shamsiye provides both secular and non-secular subjects.

Staff

Shamsiye has an approximately number of 50 staff members, which includes teaching and non-teaching staff.
The non-teaching staff includes security guards, cleaners and chefs.
